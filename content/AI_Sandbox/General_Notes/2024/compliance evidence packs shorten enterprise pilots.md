# compliance evidence packs shorten enterprise pilots

Vendors that provide structured policy, security, and audit evidence early are seeing faster pilot approvals in large organizations ([ISO 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html)).

*see also:* [[ai safety evals move into procurement checklists]] · [[managed inference startups pivot toward compliance contracts]]

## why timing matters
Pilot cycles often stall when governance teams request artifacts late and engineering must retrofit controls.

## signal braid
- Approval timelines compress with reusable evidence templates.
- Security and legal review effort declines per pilot.
- Vendor comparison becomes less narrative-driven.

## my take
Evidence quality is now a distribution advantage, not administrative overhead.
