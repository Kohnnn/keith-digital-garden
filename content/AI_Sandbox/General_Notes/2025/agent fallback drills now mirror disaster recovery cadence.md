# agent fallback drills now mirror disaster recovery cadence

Operational teams are formalizing fallback rehearsals for agent workflows to align with existing disaster recovery schedules and governance reporting cycles ([SRE workbook](https://sre.google/workbook/)).

## reliability convergence
Agent reliability testing is increasingly treated as core business continuity practice rather than feature-level QA.

## signal braid
- Recovery confidence improves with recurring drills.
- Teams discover hidden dependency assumptions earlier.
- Executive reporting gains comparable resilience metrics.

## my take
Fallback rehearsals are becoming the clearest line between symbolic readiness and actual readiness.
