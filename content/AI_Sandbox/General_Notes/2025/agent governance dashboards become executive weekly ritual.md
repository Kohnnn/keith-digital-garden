# agent governance dashboards become executive weekly ritual

As autonomous workflows expand, leadership teams are reviewing agent error rates, override frequency, and policy exceptions on weekly cadence ([OECD AI governance](https://oecd.ai/en/)).

*see also:* [[model governance now lives in release engineering]] · [[ai safety evals move into procurement checklists]]

## what changed
Governance has shifted from quarterly policy talk to operational scorecards tied to launch and budget decisions.

## operating signal
- Exception spikes trigger immediate release constraints.
- Business units compare reliability with shared definitions.
- Governance teams gain influence through measurable controls.

## my take
Executive attention is turning governance from compliance theatre into operating discipline.
