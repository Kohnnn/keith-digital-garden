# agent override protocols become training requirement for managers

As autonomous systems enter operational decision loops, companies are formalizing manager training on override triggers and escalation procedures ([CISA workforce development](https://www.cisa.gov/cybersecurity-workforce-development)).

*see also:* [[agent handoff protocols reduce enterprise escalation risk]] · [[survey of agent handoff accuracy in mixed autonomy systems]]

## people-system boundary
Override quality now depends on role clarity, confidence interpretation, and consistent incident language.

## practical signal
- Escalation quality improves after protocol training.
- Near-miss events are reported earlier.
- Teams reduce confusion during partial automation failures.

## my take
Human override readiness is becoming a first-class reliability capability.
