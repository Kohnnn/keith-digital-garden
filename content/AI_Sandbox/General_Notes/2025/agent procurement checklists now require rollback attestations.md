# agent procurement checklists now require rollback attestations

Large buyers are adding rollback-attestation requirements to agent procurement workflows, asking vendors to prove recovery speed and blast-radius limits in writing ([ISO 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html)).

*see also:* [[trust now accrues to rollback speed not launch speed]] · [[compliance evidence packs shorten enterprise pilots]]

## shift in buyer behavior
Procurement teams now treat rollback readiness as a contract-quality signal rather than an engineering detail left for post-sale implementation.

## operational signal
- Deal reviews include recovery-time evidence.
- Pilot approvals move faster when rollback playbooks are prepackaged.
- Weak rollback clarity triggers legal and security holdbacks.

## my take
Rollback evidence is becoming a default trust primitive in enterprise AI buying.
