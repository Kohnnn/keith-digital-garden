# ai procurement committees demand live control demos

Buyers increasingly ask vendors to demonstrate real-time policy enforcement and rollback behavior instead of relying on static documentation ([NIST AI RMF](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)).

*see also:* [[agent procurement checklists now require rollback attestations]] · [[compliance evidence packs shorten enterprise pilots]]

## review shift
Committees now treat control demos as proof of operational readiness under realistic failure conditions.

## market signal
- Demo quality influences shortlist outcomes.
- Legal risk decreases when controls are verified early.
- Sales cycles reward teams with rehearsed governance playbooks.

## my take
Live control demos are becoming the fastest way to convert governance claims into credibility.
