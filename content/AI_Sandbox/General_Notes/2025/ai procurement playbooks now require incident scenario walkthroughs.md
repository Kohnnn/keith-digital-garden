# ai procurement playbooks now require incident scenario walkthroughs

Buyer committees are asking vendors to run incident walkthroughs with concrete response timelines, ownership maps, and rollback evidence prior to contract approval ([ISO 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html)).

*see also:* [[contract red teaming becomes default before enterprise launch]] · [[ai procurement committees demand live control demos]]

## readiness standard
Walkthrough quality is now treated as a proxy for how teams behave under real pressure.

## market signal
- Shortlists increasingly favor operationally rehearsed teams.
- Sales cycles reward transparent incident assumptions.
- Weak escalation choreography creates procurement friction.

## my take
Scenario walkthroughs are turning governance evaluation into practical operations testing.
