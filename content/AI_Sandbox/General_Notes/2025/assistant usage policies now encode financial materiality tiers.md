# assistant usage policies now encode financial materiality tiers

Finance and risk teams are embedding materiality tiers into assistant policies so higher-impact decisions trigger stricter controls and review requirements ([IFRS resources](https://www.ifrs.org/)).

*see also:* [[agent error budgets become shared across product and finance]] · [[finops teams gain direct control of inference route policies]]

## governance alignment
Policy strictness increasingly follows potential financial impact rather than one-size-fits-all thresholds.

## operating signal
- High-impact tasks receive stronger verification layers.
- Low-impact tasks keep speed through lighter controls.
- Audit narratives improve with explicit materiality mapping.

## my take
Materiality-aware policy design is making AI governance economically coherent.
