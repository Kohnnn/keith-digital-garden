# control exception ledgers become audit default in ai teams

AI governance programs are adopting explicit exception ledgers to track who approved temporary control deviations, why they were allowed, and when they expire ([NIST AI RMF](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)).

*see also:* [[enterprise ai policy exceptions now require expiry dates]] · [[governance scorecards now drive renewal negotiations]]

## operating shift
Exception handling is moving from scattered tickets into durable records that can be audited and reviewed.

## signal braid
- Audit prep time drops when exception history is centralized.
- Policy drift is easier to detect across teams.
- Renewal decisions get clearer governance context.

## my take
Exception ledgers are a simple governance upgrade with disproportionate payoff.
