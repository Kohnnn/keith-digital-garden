# data contract breach drills become procurement differentiator

Buyers increasingly request breach-response drill evidence for data contracts, including revocation timing, notification flows, and scope containment plans ([ISO 27001](https://www.iso.org/isoiec-27001-information-security.html)).

## readiness proof
Operational rehearsal data is now weighted more heavily than generic policy statements.

## commercial signal
- Mature drill evidence shortens diligence windows.
- Legal holdbacks drop with clearer containment playbooks.
- Vendor scoring favors response repeatability.

## my take
Breach drills are becoming the governance equivalent of uptime proofs.
