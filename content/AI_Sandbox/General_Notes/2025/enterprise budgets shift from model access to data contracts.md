# enterprise budgets shift from model access to data contracts

Large organizations are increasing spend on data rights, lineage tooling, and governance contracts while model access pricing becomes more competitive ([World Economic Forum AI governance](https://www.weforum.org/agenda/archive/artificial-intelligence-and-robotics/)).

*see also:* [[procurement language is becoming product architecture]] · [[api pricing competition compresses foundation model margins]]

## budget reweighting
Teams now treat data contract quality as the long-term determinant of safety and reliability.

## practical impact
- Procurement reviews focus on lineage and revocation terms.
- Model switching gets easier under cleaner data rights.
- Governance tooling moves from optional to baseline spend.

## my take
Data contract quality is becoming the durable moat in enterprise AI programs.
