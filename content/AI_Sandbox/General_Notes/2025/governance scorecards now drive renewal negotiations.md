# governance scorecards now drive renewal negotiations

Enterprise buyers are using governance scorecards during renewal cycles to compare incident rates, control maturity, and remediation quality across vendors ([OECD AI governance](https://oecd.ai/en/)).

*see also:* [[agent governance dashboards become executive weekly ritual]] · [[assurance evidence is replacing brand reputation in enterprise deals]]

## what changed
Renewal reviews now prioritize measurable operating evidence over narrative roadmap promises.

## commercial signal
- Vendors with stable scorecards defend pricing more effectively.
- Weak governance trends trigger shorter contract terms.
- Incident transparency becomes a negotiation asset.

## my take
Governance scorecards are becoming the practical language of enterprise trust.
