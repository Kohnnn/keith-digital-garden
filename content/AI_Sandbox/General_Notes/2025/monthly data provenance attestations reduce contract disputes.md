# monthly data provenance attestations reduce contract disputes

Organizations are issuing monthly provenance attestations to confirm source integrity, rights scope, and update lineage for contracted data feeds ([W3C PROV](https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-overview/)).

*see also:* [[enterprise data licensing markets tighten around provenance guarantees]] · [[model lineage checklists become mandatory in vendor onboarding]]

## contract impact
Frequent attestations reduce ambiguity before disagreements escalate into legal disputes.

## signal braid
- Dispute resolution cycles shorten with better evidence.
- Procurement confidence rises on long-term renewals.
- Data teams improve lineage hygiene to support cadence.

## my take
Attestation rhythm matters as much as attestation format in high-stakes contracts.
