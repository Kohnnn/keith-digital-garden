# procurement reviews now score rollback communication quality

Enterprise procurement teams are evaluating rollback communication quality as part of risk diligence, including stakeholder updates, user messaging, and recovery timelines ([ISO 22301](https://www.iso.org/iso-22301-business-continuity.html)).

*see also:* [[trust now accrues to rollback speed not launch speed]] · [[incident disclosure quality is becoming a strategic asset]]

## review evolution
Buyers now judge not only whether rollback exists but whether communication under pressure is credible and consistent.

## signal braid
- Vendor trust improves with clear rollback narratives.
- Legal teams reduce holdbacks when comms quality is proven.
- Poor communication increases perceived operational risk.

## my take
Rollback communication quality is becoming an enterprise buying signal, not a PR concern.
