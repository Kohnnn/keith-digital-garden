# quarterly trust drills become product launch gate

Teams are running recurring trust drills that test refusal clarity, rollback messaging, and escalation readiness before major AI launches proceed ([Nielsen Norman Group](https://www.nngroup.com/)).

*see also:* [[pilot to production gates now include user trust telemetry]] · [[automation trust now depends on visible fail safe design]]

## launch discipline
Trust drills convert abstract readiness claims into observable behaviors under controlled stress.

## signal braid
- Launch decisions are less optimistic and more evidence-based.
- Cross-team coordination improves before incidents occur.
- User-facing failure handling quality rises over time.

## my take
Trust drills are becoming a practical launch filter for responsible product velocity.
