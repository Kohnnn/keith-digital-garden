# enterprise search vendors reprice after copilot bundling

Bundled productivity copilots are pressuring standalone enterprise search vendors to reframe pricing around differentiated workflow outcomes rather than query volume alone ([Forrester search and knowledge management](https://www.forrester.com/)).

*see also:* [[enterprise ai copilot renewals split by realized roi]] · [[cloud software guidance favors workflow incumbents]]

## scene cut
Procurement teams are consolidating contracts, favoring suites with integrated identity and compliance controls unless specialists show measurable domain lift.

## signal braid
- Standalone tools face higher proof requirements.
- Price per seat pressure increases in broad deployments.
- Domain specific relevance quality becomes key moat.

## my take
Search vendors now win by proving embedded workflow impact, not by selling generic retrieval capability.
