# enterprise vector database contracts shift toward usage floors

Enterprise buyers and vector database vendors are converging on pricing structures with usage floors and capacity bands to improve budget predictability ([Databricks data + ai trends](https://www.databricks.com/resources/research)).

*see also:* [[vector database consolidation follows budget pressure]] · [[cloud software guidance favors workflow incumbents]]

## scene cut
Procurement teams prefer clearer downside limits, while vendors seek commitment signals amid heavy competition and falling unit prices.

## signal braid
- Contract complexity rises with hybrid deployment options.
- Buyers negotiate stronger portability clauses.
- Usage floors stabilize revenue but increase entry friction.

## my take
The pricing model now reflects maturity pressure: fewer experiments, more procurement discipline.
