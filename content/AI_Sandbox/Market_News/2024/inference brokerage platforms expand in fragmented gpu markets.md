# inference brokerage platforms expand in fragmented gpu markets

Intermediary platforms that aggregate inference capacity across providers are gaining traction as organizations seek flexible access under fragmented GPU supply conditions ([NVIDIA ecosystem overview](https://www.nvidia.com/en-us/)).

## scene cut
Buyers are trading absolute control for faster provisioning and price optionality across regional supply pockets.

## signal braid
- Brokerage volume rises in bursty demand scenarios.
- Price discovery improves with multi-provider visibility.
- Reliability risk shifts to broker orchestration quality.

## my take
