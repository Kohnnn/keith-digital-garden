# managed inference startups pivot toward compliance contracts

Managed inference startups are repositioning around auditability, residency, and governance guarantees as enterprise buyers prioritize risk controls in contract decisions ([ISO 27001 overview](https://www.iso.org/isoiec-27001-information-security.html)).

*see also:* [[ai safety evals move into procurement checklists]] · [[cloud software guidance favors workflow incumbents]]

## scene cut
Sales materials increasingly foreground compliance artifacts, incident response commitments, and logging capabilities ahead of benchmark speed claims.

## signal braid
- Contract win rates improve where controls are pre certified.
- Buyer due diligence cycles shorten with clear evidence packs.
- Performance differentiation alone is less decisive in regulated accounts.

## my take
Enterprise adoption keeps rewarding providers that reduce governance burden at purchase time.
