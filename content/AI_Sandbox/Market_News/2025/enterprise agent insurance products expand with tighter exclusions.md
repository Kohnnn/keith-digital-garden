# enterprise agent insurance products expand with tighter exclusions

Insurers are expanding AI-specific enterprise coverage offerings while tightening exclusions tied to weak controls, poor auditability, and missing rollback procedures ([Marsh cyber reports](https://www.marsh.com/us/services/cyber-risk.html)).
