# enterprise data licensing markets tighten around provenance guarantees

Data licensing negotiations are placing stronger emphasis on provenance verification, scope clarity, and revocation rights in response to higher AI usage intensity ([W3C provenance community](https://www.w3.org/community/prov/)).

*see also:* [[enterprise budgets shift from model access to data contracts]] · [[procurement language is becoming product architecture]]

## scene cut
Buyers are demanding clearer chain-of-custody records and update obligations before integrating third-party data into production agents.

## signal braid
- Provenance guarantees become premium contract terms.
- Unclear rights increase legal holdback in deployments.
- Revocation mechanisms influence architecture design choices.

## my take
Provenance quality is becoming the most expensive and important part of external data value.
