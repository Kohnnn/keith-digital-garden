# W3 2025: Bitcoin at $100k, Reserves Drop, Policy Front-Running

Market rates stable with declining term premium and upward yield curve, pricing few cuts; Bitcoin holds bull support near $100k amid reserves plunge to $2.89T.[1][2][3][web:6][web:7]

**Rates & Curve**  
- Term premium down, curve steepening mildly—well-behaved, skeptical on cut pricing.[1][2][3]  
- Needs bull flattener in long-end for equity highs; fatigue evident.[3]  

**Liquidity Squeeze**  
- Bank reserves sharp drop to $2.89T, RRP ~$100B—tightening signal, watch drain.[1][3]  
- [[Liquidity Risks]] loom if repo runoff accelerates.[1][3]  

**Equities Rotation**  
- Small caps (IWM, IJR) beat S&P 500, not at ATHs yet.[1]  
- Earnings guidance sours: more negatives in consumer discretionary, materials.[1]  

**Bitcoin Strength**  
- Approaches $100k post-CPI, sweeps range lows, bull support band intact.[web:6][web:7][web:8]  
- Defies fatigue, supports risk thesis.[web:6][web:7]  

**Policy Front-Run**  
- Tariffs/inflation spur retail imports (port strike dodge).[4][6]  
- Disinflation narrative vs. isolationism risks.[2]  

> **>info** Stable rates + BTC underpin risk assets; pragmatic watch on liquidity/policy. Thesis: "Stable rates and Bitcoin strength support risk assets amid liquidity watch and policy risks."[1][2][3][web:6]
