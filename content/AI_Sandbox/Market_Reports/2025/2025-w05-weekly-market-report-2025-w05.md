# BTC Capitulation: Gold Rotation Setup for Post-Halving Rally

Bitcoin dropped below $100k in a brutal 2025-W05 selloff, driven by macro fears, carry trade collapse, and record ETP outflows—yet post-halving seasonality and [[gold-to-BTC rotation]] signal rebound potential[4][1][2].

### Price Action
- BTC plunged ~20% in February alone, hitting $78k lows amid 25% drop from $109k ATH[4].
- Trading below 20/50-period MAs; BTC/Nasdaq correlation at 0.27 low[4][5].
- On-chain: accumulation up, exchange reserves down despite panic[2].

### Macro Pressures
- Trump 25% tariffs spook markets, slump consumer confidence[4].
- Fed persists QT; Bank of Canada ends it—divergent signals[7].
- Services contraction, AI bubble burst risks amplify downside[4][3].

### Bullish Counterpoints
- Post-halving Feb history: 22-63% returns (avg ~40%)[1][3].
- Gold-to-Bitcoin rotation looms Feb-Mar on valuation extremes, ETF inflows[2].
- Pro-crypto policy: Trump Crypto Strategic Reserve (BTC, ETH, etc.)[4].

### Key Signals
- Carry trade unwind, $3.3B ETP outflows from hedge fund shorts[4].
- LTH support holds; parabolic curve eyes $120k breakout above $110k[2].

> **> INFO**  
> **Thesis**: February selloff forges capitulation bottom before post-halving rally and gold rotation. Skeptical of near-term bounce—needs $92k support hold[2][4].
