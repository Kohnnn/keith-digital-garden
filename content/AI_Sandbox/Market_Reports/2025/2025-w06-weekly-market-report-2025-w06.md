# 2025-W06: Tech Crash, BTC Dominance, TSLA Support Test

Tech-heavy markets corrected sharply amid **bubble fears** and **BTC dominance** at 64%, but labor resilience hints at rebound potential.[1][2]

**TSLA Breakdown**  
- Monthly drop of **24.2%** to $292.98 by Feb 28, from $394 highs.[1]  
- Key support at **340-390**; hold could target **500+** in 3-5 months.[2]  
- Risk: break below 340 eyes **246**.[2]  

**BTC Dominance Surge**  
- Hit **64%**, delaying [[Alt Season]] as BTC outperforms.  
- Altcoins lag without dominance decline.[8]  

**ETH Uncertainty**  
- No clear bottom; struggles persist in choppy conditions.[4]  

**Tech/AI Sector Pain**  
- **NVIDIA** and AI leaders crash despite hype from DeepSeek, OpenAI, xAI.[1][6]  
- Broader market called **biggest bubble** ever, overvaluation warnings.[5]  

**Labor Backdrop**  
- US unemployment at **4%**, resilient signal amid volatility.[9]
