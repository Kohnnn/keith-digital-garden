# Tariff Squeeze: Yield Flatten & BTC Dom Week 09

**2025-W09 (Feb 24-Mar 2):** Tariffs flatten yield curve and contract services amid BTC dominance rise, pressuring alts and small caps.[1][4]

### Yield Curve Flattens
- **3-month to 10-year spread at 10bps**, eyeing second inversion pre-FOMC.[1]
- Signals **recession risk**; skeptical of soft landing narratives.[1]
- Ties to [[Yield Curve Inversion]] history.

### Services Contraction
- **Services PMI <50** confirms economic pullback, mirroring sentiment drop.[1]
- Existing home sales -4.9%, Michigan inflation expectations at 3.5%.[1]
- Spillover risk to broader economy; pragmatic cut exposure.[1]

### Tariff Pressures
- Durable goods inflation at **1.37%**, front-running inventories, domestic shifts.[2][4]
- **Goods volatility** from imports; watch substitution plays.[2][4]

### CPI Steady, Caps Diverge
- Super core YoY **2.36%**, headline nears 2%, core ~2.6%.[4]
- Small caps (IWM) **-6.6% off highs**; SPY holds on earnings.[4]

### BTC Dominance Cycle
- Targeting **66%**, alts (ETH, SOL) weaken vs BTC.[web:10-12]
- See [[BTC Dom Cycle]]; pragmatic: BTC first, alts later.
