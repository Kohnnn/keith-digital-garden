# W10 2025: Crash Fears vs Crypto Cycles

Mixed signals dominated: stock downside pressure offset crypto cycle maturation and policy tailwinds.[1]

### Stock Volatility
- Brian Shannon flags key indices breaking supports, yet technical setups hint rebounds.[1][2]
- Sasha Yanshin sees early 2025 crash underway from overvaluation, economic slowdown.[1]

### Crypto Cycles
- BTC's multiple $100k crosses signal maturity risks; Cowen questions right vs left translation.[3][4]
- ETH butterfly pattern projects multi-month consolidation before upside.[5]
- Mixed sentiment: BTC tests levels post-$100k, ETH lags relative strength.[1]

### Policy Shifts
- Meldrum's outlook: tax debates favor capital over labor, boosting asset appeal.[6][7]

### Key Signals
- BTC crosses vs SPX breakdowns; consolidation likely for majors.[1][2][3]
