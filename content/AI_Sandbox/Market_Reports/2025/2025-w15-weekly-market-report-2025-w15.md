# Weekly Market Report 2025-W15

### Tariff Carnage
- **S&P500 tanks 20%**: Direct hit from tariff rollout, signaling broad US economic damage.[6c1643976170a37d898245388852ea5b8a1241cd]
- Sasha Yanshin calls it "destroying the US economy"—pragmatic view: overblown rhetoric, but volumes confirm pain.[0d3ef18ac199343f207dd72b89b965fe6d04118c]

### Policy Reprieve
- Trump announces **90-day tariff pause** April 10: Brief rally potential, but skepticism high on follow-through.[5c839b6f712a4287eb0a0955080c8b473a93a23c]
- Ties to [[Note on Tariff Cycles]]—echoes past pauses that faded fast.

### Inflation Eases
- Drops to **2.4%**: Bullish macro signal amid equity rout; Fed pivot whispers grow.[b8992c5f712a4287eb0a0955080c8b473a93a23c]
- Pragmatic: Not enough to offset tariff drag yet.
