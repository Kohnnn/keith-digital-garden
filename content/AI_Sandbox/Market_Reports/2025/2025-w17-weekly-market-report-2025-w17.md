# BTC April Surge: Breakout to $94K & Altseason Setup

Bitcoin closed the week of 2025-W17 at $94,011, up 12.49% for April amid ETF inflows, institutional buys, and on-chain strength—yet dominance near 70% hints at altcoin rotation if rejection plays out.[1][4]

**Price Action**  
- BTC broke above $90K, 50-day MA, and falling wedge; RSI in 60s, Ichimoku Cloud breached.[1][2][4][5]  
- Weekly volume +73%, bullish momentum divergence; 16.7M BTC in profit—a historical bull precursor.[1][4][5]  
- MicroStrategy stacked 15,355 BTC ($1.42B avg $92,737) Apr 21-27, corporate FOMO intact.[1]

**On-Chain & Sentiment**  
- Daily active addresses +4%, tx fees $12,500/block, stablecoins +$4.2B, CME OI record 120K contracts.[1]  
- Fear & Greed peaked 72 (Greed) Apr 23, cooled to 60; short-term holders out early.[4]  
- BTC dominance ~70%, eyeing alt rotation (AI, DeFi, NFTs, memecoins) on peak/rejection.[1]

**Ethereum & DeFi**  
- ETH -4.25% to $1,765, but 92K+ net Binance inflows signal accumulation.[1]  
- DeFi TVL steady: Aave $3.4B, Compound $2.7B—underperformance masks strength.[1]

**Risks (Skeptical Lens)**  
- False breakout looms; stops at $87,500 or <$88,205 targets $77K.[1][3]  
- Leverage traps via basis spreads, funding rates; resistance $95K-$100K.[1][5]  
- Greed cooldown questions sustainability—[[False Breakout Note]] vibes strong.[4]
