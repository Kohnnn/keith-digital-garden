# BTC Cycle Top: Bear Digestion Begins (W23 2025)

Bitcoin's 2023-2025 bull cycle has topped, entering late-cycle digestion like mid-2019, with tactical rallies masking a bearish grind ahead.[5]

## Cycle Status
- **BTC bear market** since October 2025: apathy-driven, 162-day duration (shorter than priors), no retail euphoria.[3][6]
- Dominance >60%, per Cowen's Step 2; Q4 peak passed per post-halving norms.[1]
- Late digestion phase: rallies tactical, not sustainable.[5]

## Price Targets
- **BTC correction**: multi-month drop to $90K-$95K (20/21-week SMA/EMA band), lows ~Aug/Sep after mid-June weakness.[2]
- Support break below $100K imminent.[2]
- [[Cycle Top Indicators]] align with supply/profit metrics showing exhaustion.[3]

## Alts & ETH
- **Alt capitulation**: ALT/BTC to range lows; selective rotation (e.g., Monero privacy plays).[2][3]
- ETH "bleeding down" to $2K fair value; potential $2,140 June low sweep sans Fed easing.[3]
- ETH/BTC weakness persists.[3]

## Macro Backdrop
- US jobs strong (4.2% unemployment, low claims): mutes recession risk, delays easing, prolongs decline.[3]
- No aggressive Fed cuts expected.[3]
