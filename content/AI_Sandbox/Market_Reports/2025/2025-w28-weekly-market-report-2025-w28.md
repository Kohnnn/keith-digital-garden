# 2025-W28: Tech Records Amid Hawkish Fed

S&P 500 up 2.2% in July, third straight monthly gain to records on AI earnings and low VIX (~17), but narrow breadth and Fed hawkishness limit breadth.[1][2][3]

**Index Moves**
- S&P 500: +2.2%, 10 new highs, YTD +7.78%[1]
- Nasdaq: 4th straight gain, large-cap led[2]
- Russell 2000: +1.7% modest[2]
- Dow: +0.1% flat[2]

**Sector Standouts**
- IT: >5% gains, Magnificent Seven drove ~all S&P rise (45% stocks down)[1][2][3]
- Energy/Financials/Communications: EPS surprises +12.7%/+10.8%/+9.0%[1][2]
- Healthcare: -3.4% drag[1][2]

**Earnings & Macro**
- Q2: +7.3% surprise (above 10yr avg), AI/trade ease/resilient data[1][2]
- Jobs: June beat, unemployment 4.1%, cool CPI/PPI; weak housing/claims[1][2]
- Fed: Hawkish hold, no Sept cut; Trump-Powell tension, stablecoin bill passed[1][2]

**Key Signals**
- AI momentum, easing tariffs post-Liberation Day[1][2]
- Bullish July seasonality (up 9 straight yrs), targets 7200-7500 EOY[2][8]

> **> note** Narrow breadth risks snap-back; small-caps lag signals rotation doubts.[3]
