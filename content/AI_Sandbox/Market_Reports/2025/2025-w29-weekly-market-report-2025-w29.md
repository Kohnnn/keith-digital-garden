# BTC Bear Grind: ETH Bleeds to $2K Fair Value

**W2025-W29 (Jul 14-20):** BTC digests bear phase above support; ETH weakens vs BTC amid dominance rise, no QE pivot yet.[130def4d165091191c097e4ed7b0d688c56792e1][bf1ed9d0af559bd514f2ff1d1d831cf154c483c9]

### BTC Bear Cycle
- Ongoing bear since Oct 2025, 162 days vs prior 1000+ cycles, apathy phase.[a1951efc8586f49acd71201993117c5c1b5fd9d0]
- Holds **bull market support** (20W SMA/21W EMA); Q4 rally if intact, per history.[130def4d165091191c097e4ed7b0d688c56792e1]
- Dominance rising in bear; institutions buy BTC ETFs ($332M), sell ETH ($135M).[bf1ed9d0af559bd514f2ff1d1d831cf154c483c9]

### ETH Derivative Decay
- Acts as BTC shadow; **ETH/BTC** weak, regression targets ~$2000 fair value.[130def4d165091191c097e4ed7b0d688c56792e1][18b441265cb7df51bbe6d3b51edf34cf8ed3ffef]

### Macro & Policy Stall
