# BTC Peak & Alt Rotation: 2025-W31 Market Pause

BTC hit ATH ~$124,500 before 6-7.7% correction to ~$108,250; ETH surged 18% to new ATH >$4,900 amid rotation.[1][4][5]

## Price Action
- **BTC**: Higher low at $115K support post-pullback; bearish momentum weakening, golden cross intact on daily.[2][3]
- **ETH**: DeFi strength + ETF inflows drove gains as BTC dominance dropped 72%→60%.[1][2][4][5]
- Equities (S&P/NASDAQ) at records; gold beat summer norms despite volatility.[4][5]

## Flows & Sentiment
- BTC ETFs: -$0.75B outflows (worst Aug 1); ETH ETFs massive inflows.[1][4]
- Global BTC ETF inflows persist; Fear & Greed at 62/100 (greed); volatility decline aids institutions.[2][4]
- Fed held 4.5% rates, unemployment 4.2%, inflation sticky—rate cuts revised.[5]

## Key Signals
- BTC higher low + dominance decline = alt season start.[2]
- ETH ATH/ETFs confirm rotation; macro resilient.[1][4][5]
- Declining vol supports adoption.[4]

## Risks
- <$110K-100K breaks deeper correction on hawkish Fed/tariffs.[2][3]
- Policy uncertainty, overdue selling post-highs.[1][3]
