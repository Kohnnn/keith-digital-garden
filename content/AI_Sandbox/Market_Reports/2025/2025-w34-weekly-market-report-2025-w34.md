# Week 34: BTC Support Tests & Stock Put Risks

BTC tested **bull market support bands** while stocks eyed profit-taking; crypto held bull structure amid ETH strength.[2][3]

**BTC Cycle Pressure**  
- Dominance trends key watchpoint as support band tests loom in bull context.[2][3][4]  
- Price action across multiple timeframes signals caution on breakdowns.[1]  
- August correction from $124k ATH to ~$108k fits cooldown pattern post-rally.[1][2]  

**ETH/Alt Momentum**  
- ETH eyes **all-time highs** vs BTC, backed by ETF inflows and pair dynamics.[3][6][7]  
- Hit $4,948 peak Aug 24 before pullback; outperformance signals alt rotation.[1][2][5]  
- BTC dominance drop to ~58% aids alt gains, but vulnerable to reversals.[1][3]  

**Stock Market Headwinds**  
- **Three put problems** cap upside: overextension risks in Nasdaq 100.  
- Profit-taking scenarios build amid questions on further gains.  

**Geopolitical Sentiment**  
- Indirect military-industrial complex talks weigh on risk assets.
