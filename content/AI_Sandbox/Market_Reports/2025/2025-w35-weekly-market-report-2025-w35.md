# Weekly Market Report 2025-W35

Crypto markets rotated from **Bitcoin** pause to **Ethereum** strength amid mixed ETF flows and altcoin gains, per August reports[1][2][3].

## Bitcoin: ATH then pullback
- Hit fresh **ATH ~$124k** mid-month before -6.5% drop to ~$108k, pressured by tariffs and macro uncertainty[1][3].
- **BTC dominance** fell 5.9% to ~58%, signaling rotation[1].
- ETF outflows totaled -$0.75B; September historically weak (-3.31% avg)[3].
- Support band holds, but dubious speculation flagged[[Bitcoin Speculation]][7e7a1f0299dc28b8b6ff72d048bd8dbdcf62bf15].

## Ethereum: New highs, institutional shift
- Surged 18% to **ATH $4,948** on Aug 24, then ~$4,400; ETF inflows +$3.8B[2][3].
- Outperformed BTC on undervaluation, legislation, DeFi beta[2].
- Institutions rotating in via ETFs; ETH/BTC eyes 0.08 historical peak[2].
- Dubious speculation noted despite momentum[[Ethereum Speculation]][0a4d0015fa50774c7b4116fba633f6fc44fec639].

## Altcoins & DeFi: Rotation underway
- **Altseason** signals: ETH, Solana +14%; Oracles, Liquid Staking led MC growth[3][4].
- CRO doubled on treasury plans; BNB, MYX, OKB strong; memecoins volatile (YZY -83%)[2].
- XRP risk metric highlighted[[XRP Risks]][6a2227035be69a62bb1920e17b69700010ae862b].

## Macro overlays
- Equity highs, persistent inflation; Fed cut odds high post-Powell[3].
- Trade tensions eased; global growth forecast 3.0%[3].
