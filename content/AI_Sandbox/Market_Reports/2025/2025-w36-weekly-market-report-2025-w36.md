# 2025-W36 Market Report: BTC Q4 Peak Setup Amid Sep Consolidation

BTC held key supports in a choppy week, aligning with forecasts for September pullback before Q4 rally to $131K-$154K[1][2][3].

## BTC Price Action
- Started week ~$108K-$109K post-August decline, rebounded 2.66% to $112K range on weak jobs data[2].
- Tested $109K-$111K support band; no break of **20-week SMA**[[Bull Market Support]] or **50-week MA** (~$100K)[1][3].
- Dominance ~66%, capital consolidating in BTC over alts[3].

## Key Signals
- Hold above 20-week SMA signals Q4 rally; September low formation eyes October ATH[1][2].
- Diminishing returns vs. prior cycles; retail muted near highs[1][3].
- Silver eyes ATHs, precious metals strong[Sources].

## Altcoin Caution
- Ethereum dubious speculation; brief rally to $5K-$7K possible before 2026 weakness[3].
- Thesis: BTC Sep pullback to supports, then Q4 peak; alts lag[[Diminishing Returns]].

## Macro Risks
- Unemployment at 4.3%, headwind for risk assets[Sources].
- High Fed rates limit liquidity[3].
