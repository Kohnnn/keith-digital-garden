# 2025-W38 Crypto Report: Dips Hold, Alts Rotate

BTC tested $109K-$110K support after $124K highs, amid $1.65B-$3.45B liquidations—market shed $160B but clawed back to $3.95T cap[1][2][3].

**BTC/ETH Price Action**
- BTC plunged below $111K on panic, held $109K-$111K band post-Fed cut rally to $115K[1][2][3].
- ETH dipped under $4K (3.6-8.15% monthly), pivoted at $4K with DeFi TVL eyeing $4,474-$4,600[1][2][3][5].
- Fear & Greed at 28 (fear); deleveraging reversed mid-month gains[2][3].

**Market Cap & Dominance**
- Cap swung $3.9T-$4T, down early but +2-5% rebound[1][2][3][5].
- BTC dominance 57.68%; capital rotated to alts[1][3].

**Altcoin Rotation**
- AVAX +133%, BNB ATH $1,080, DOGE/XRP ETF launches[1][2].
- DePIN/RWAs surged; signals sector maturity over BTC reliance[1][2].

**Institutions & Leverage**
- ETF outflows: $418M BTC, $795M ETH—yet OI spiked $7B to $78B, whales accumulating[1][2].
- Mixed: maturity evident, but over-leverage risks persist[1].

**Macro Catalysts**
- Fed 25bps cut Sep 17 fueled rally, FOMC stabilized post-deleveraging[2][3].
- 'Uptober' optimism tempers historical Sep/Oct weakness[[Uptober Patterns]].
