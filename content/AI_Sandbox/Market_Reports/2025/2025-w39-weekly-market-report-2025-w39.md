# BTC September Test, Q4 Rally Ahead (W39 2025)

**Bitcoin held bull market support amid typical September weakness, setting up potential Q4 rally to $131k-$154k if key levels defend.**[1][2]

### BTC Support Band Tested
- Classic post-halving September dip: price action probed **20-week SMA** and **21-week EMA**, historical bull floor.[1][2][8]
- Held above **50-week MA (~$100k)**; weekly close below flips bullish bias early-cycle.[2]
- Volatility cluster: $107k-$109k range early week, recovery to $113k month-end.[5]

### Q4 Cycle Peak Outlook
- **Cowen forecast**: $131k-$154k Q4 top, mirroring past cycles (Sep dip, then rally).[1]
- Polarized expert calls: Brandt eyes $125k-$150k end-Sep, others $118k-$128k avg.[2][6]
- Institutional buys (e.g., 850 BTC at $117k avg) signal accumulation under noise.[5]

### Broader Market Digestion
- **S&P 500** direction unclear, potential consolidation; equities late-cycle like mid-2019.[4]
- **Silver** nears ATHs, precious metals strength amid risk-off.[6]
- Fed Sep cut risks bond yield spike, pressuring BTC/alts.[6]

### Key Risks
- Break <$100k triggers deeper correction, ends bull bias.[2][4]
- ETF outflows + futures liquidations cap rallies as tactical, not structural.[5]
