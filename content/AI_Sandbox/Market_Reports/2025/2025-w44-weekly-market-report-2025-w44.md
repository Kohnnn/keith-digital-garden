# BTC Cycle Peak Watch: Q4 2025 Signals

Bitcoin traded around **$109,250** in 2025-W44, testing resistance amid rising dominance and QT end signals[1][2][4].

### Price Snapshots
- **BTC**: $109,250; resistance $110k-$111.5k, support $106.5k-$108k. Close >$109.5k needed for momentum[1].
- **ETH**: $3,864; pivot $3,850-$3,880 holds, but [[Head & Shoulders]] threatens $3,750-$3,780[1].
- **SOL**: $187.90; strongest performer, eyeing $190-$195 resistance, support $180-$184[1].

### Cycle Thesis
BTC nears late-2025 peak per Cowen: post-election rally into Q4 top, then 2026 midterm correction mirroring 2014/2018/2022[2][3][4]. Dominance at **66%** consolidates capital, alts lag[2][3].

### Key Signals
- Dominance surges into Dec; 50-week MA (~$103k) as cycle support—break confirms top[2][4].
- Fed QT ends Dec 2025 (cycle midpoint), lifts peak odds to 50%[4].
- Total mcap fair value $4.4T, eyes $10T long-term[3].
- ETH reclaim $3,900 negates bearish pattern[1].

### Risks
- BTC rejection at $109.5k-$110k tests $106.5k[1].
- Weekly close <50-week MA seals peak[4].
- ETH < $3,850 validates H&S to $3,760[1].
- 2026 decline baked in historically[2][4].
