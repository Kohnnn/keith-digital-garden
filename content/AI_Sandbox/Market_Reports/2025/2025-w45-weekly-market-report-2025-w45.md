# November 2025 Market Breakdown: Volatility Rises, Concentration Cracks

S&P 500 eked out ~0.1-0.25% gain amid sharp mid-month drops and late rebound, masking rotation from megacap AI to value/healthcare[1][2][5][6].

## Index Breakdowns
- S&P 500 down 5.7% from October high, Nasdaq-100/Composite lagged (-1.5% to -3.1%), VIX surged signaling volatility[1][2][3].
- Red engulfing weekly candle eyes 587 or 21k downside; dollar strength adds deflationary pressure[1].
- Small-caps/Russell 2000, equal-weight S&P up ~1-2%, outpacing caps at 16% fair value discount[2][5][6].

## AI Concentration Cracks
- Mega-caps drove YTD but cooled: tech -4.3%, Nvidia -7.1%, SMCI -33%, Oracle -20% on capex/debt woes[1][2][3][5].
- Circular AI deals questioned; sustainability doubts amid tariff uncertainty[3].
- See [[AI Capex Risks]] for deeper dive.

## Asset Rotations
- Value/small-caps/REITs/bonds beat megacaps; healthcare +9.3% led[1][2][6].
- Gold/copper momentum positive, Bitcoin worst 2025 asset battling 50W MA[1][6].
- Bonds drifted lower yields (4.02%), commodities/USD fell on AI/tariff fears[3][6].

## Trading Signals
- Ensembles beat S&P risk-adjusted (1.7 vs 0.84 ratio) despite losses; breadth concerns linger[3][4].
- Whipsaw volatility hits strategies, hedges like bonds/gold fail[4].
