# 2025-W46: AI Jitters, Fed Hopes, BTC Reclaim

Volatile week amid November's late rally on **Fed cut bets**, masking **Mag7 weakness** and sector shifts.[1][2]

## Equities: Narrow Gains, Rotation Accelerates
- **S&P 500 +0.13-0.25%**, NASDAQ -1.51%; late rebound on rate cut hopes.[1][2]
- **Mag7 underperformed** on AI valuation fears, bubble chatter.[1][3]
- **Defensives led**: Health care, comms, energy, staples, utilities up; tech/discretionary/industrials lagged.[1][2][3]
- Small-caps at **16% discount**, attractive vs. megacaps.[4]

## Fixed Income: Yields Dip on Labor Softness
- 10yr Treasury -8bps, 2yr -5bps; signals **Dec Fed cut likely**.[1]
- Supports equities amid softening jobs data.[1]

## Crypto: Bitcoin Bullish Breakout
- BTC **reclaimed 50-week MA**, dominance set to surge into Dec.[3]
- [[Bitcoin Dominance]] metrics bullish; Fear & Greed noted.[3]
- ETH dubious speculation per analysis.

## Earnings & Global: Resilient but Mixed
- Banks/megatech solid; consumer sheets strong despite bifurcation.[4]
- Europe/Asia up on Fed hopes; China GDP cut to 4.5% on trade woes.[1]
