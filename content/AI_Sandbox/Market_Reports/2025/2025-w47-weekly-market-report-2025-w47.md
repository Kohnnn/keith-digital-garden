# BTC Cycle Peak Q4 2025: Dominance Rises Ahead of Bear

BTC peaked late 2025 per cycles, now correcting post-November drawdown from $126K to ~$91K amid ETF outflows and macro stress[1][2][4].

## BTC Price Action
- Fell below **50-week MA** (~$100K), confirming topping process[1].
- Recent bounce from November lows ~$80K to $91-95K, testing 200-day SMA ~$104K short-term[1][3][4].
- Dominance at 58.5-66%, capital fleeing alts despite brief rotation signals[1][3].

## Altcoin Bleed
- ETH/SOL face short rallies (ETH $5-7K) then 2026 weakness[1][2].
- Sector dispersion: memecoins/NFTs hit hardest in 20-30% BTC drawdown[2].
- ETH down 21%, network resilient despite $55B DeFi TVL drop[2][5].

## Macro Headwinds
- Unemployment at **4.4%**, tying to bearish midterm outlook.
- Fed tightness curbs liquidity; ETF outflows $3.5B BTC/$1.4B ETH[2].
- Equities mixed: META buy near $550, but Nasdaq -1.5%[6].

## Key Risks
- 2026 bear to BTC $60-70K (200-week SMA), alts weaker[1][4].
- Low retail FOMO supports peak narrative, no systemic break yet[1][2].
