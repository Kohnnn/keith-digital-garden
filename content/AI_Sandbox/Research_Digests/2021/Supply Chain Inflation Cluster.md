This cluster connects port congestion, factory slowdowns, and shipping delays to inflation pressure. The common thread is bottlenecks in physical flow, not just demand.

## signals
- Shipping delays translated into higher consumer prices.
- Logistics became a primary inflation channel.
- Production throttles amplified scarcity.
- Bottlenecks persisted longer than expected.
- Inventory strategy shifted toward resilience.
