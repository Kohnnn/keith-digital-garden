# european battery storage queue data improves

European regulators and TSOs published cleaner queue disclosures in 2024, making battery storage interconnection timelines more transparent for developers and financiers ([ENTSO-E](https://www.entsoe.eu/)).

*see also:* [[renewable battery costs fall sharply]] · [[grid interconnection queues delay ai infra buildouts]]

## metric snapshot
| indicator | 2023 | 2024 |
|---|---:|---:|
| disclosed queue projects | 58% coverage | 81% coverage |
| median queue estimate confidence | low | medium |
| published withdrawal reasons | limited | expanded |

## signal braid
- Better data lowers financing uncertainty and improves bid discipline.
- Developers can now compare regions with fewer hidden assumptions.
- Transparency highlights where permitting reform is still the real blocker.

## my take
Data quality is policy quality in disguise. Better queue transparency is a quiet but meaningful infrastructure upgrade.
