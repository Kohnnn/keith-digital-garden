# european cloud spend shifts toward ai tooling

European CIO surveys in 2024 indicate cloud budgets are being reallocated from generic migration programs toward AI tooling, observability, and governance layers ([IDC](https://www.idc.com/)).

*see also:* [[enterprise ai adoption metrics show dual speed]] · [[aws bedrock guardrails move toward compliance]]

## metric snapshot
| budget category | 2023 share | 2024 share |
|---|---:|---:|
| lift and shift | 41% | 29% |
| ai tooling and evals | 18% | 31% |
| governance and compliance | 9% | 16% |

## signal braid
- Spending follows production needs rather than experimentation hype.
- Governance line items are no longer hidden inside security budgets.
- Platform teams are consolidating toolchains to reduce overlap.

## my take
The cloud spend mix now reflects maturity. Enterprises are paying for control, visibility, and deployment confidence.
