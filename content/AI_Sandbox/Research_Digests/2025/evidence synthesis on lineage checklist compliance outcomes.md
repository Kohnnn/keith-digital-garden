# evidence synthesis on lineage checklist compliance outcomes

Teams using structured lineage checklists report better audit preparation speed and clearer post-incident attribution pathways in regulated deployments ([ISO 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html)).

*see also:* [[model lineage checklists become mandatory in vendor onboarding]] · [[safety claims without eval lineage are just marketing]]

## evidence map
- Checklist completeness predicts audit friction.
- Lineage gaps correlate with longer incident investigations.
- Standardized fields improve cross-team comparability.

## method boundary
Outcomes depend on checklist freshness and integration into release workflows.

## my take
Lineage checklists are becoming one of the highest-ROI compliance habits.
