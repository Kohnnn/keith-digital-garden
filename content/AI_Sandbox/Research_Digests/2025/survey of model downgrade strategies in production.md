# survey of model downgrade strategies in production

Operational case studies show model downgrade strategies are most effective when coupled with pre-defined trigger thresholds and user-impact communication standards ([SRE practices](https://sre.google/)).

*see also:* [[model downgrade playbooks reduce outage panic]] · [[meta review of agent rollback benchmark methodologies]]

## evidence stack
- Trigger ambiguity delays recovery in many incidents.
- Gradual downgrade ladders reduce sudden quality shocks.
- Communication quality influences user trust retention.

## method boundary
Downgrade evaluations should include both technical and user-perception outcomes.

## my take
Downgrade quality is a socio-technical capability, not only a routing decision.
