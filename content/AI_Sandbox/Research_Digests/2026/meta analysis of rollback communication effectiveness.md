# meta analysis of rollback communication effectiveness

Research synthesis indicates that clear rollback communication reduces user confusion, shortens support spikes, and improves confidence during degraded operations ([CISA incident response](https://www.cisa.gov/resources-tools/services/incident-response)).

*see also:* [[procurement reviews now score rollback communication quality]] · [[survey of model downgrade strategies in production]]

## evidence map
- Timely scope disclosure lowers reputational damage.
- Concrete recovery milestones improve stakeholder alignment.
- Ambiguous messaging prolongs incident impact.

## method boundary
Communication quality should be evaluated alongside technical recovery metrics for full incident assessment.

## my take
Rollback communication is now a core operational competency, not a post-incident afterthought.
