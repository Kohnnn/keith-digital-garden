# study synthesis on provenance attestation reliability

Recent studies and market practice suggest provenance attestations are most reliable when schemas are standardized and refresh cadence is enforced contractually ([W3C PROV](https://www.w3.org/TR/prov-overview/)).

*see also:* [[monthly data provenance attestations reduce contract disputes]] · [[provenance verification startups partner with licensing marketplaces]]

## evidence stack
- Inconsistent schemas reduce attestation comparability.
- Delayed attestations increase dispute risk.
- Automated verification improves trust in large ecosystems.

## method boundary
Reliability metrics should include timeliness, completeness, and verification reproducibility.

## my take
Attestation reliability is becoming a contract-grade operational metric.
