# structured incident packets automate postmortem drafting

Incident pipelines are generating structured packets with timeline, policy state, affected scopes, and remediation actions to accelerate postmortem drafting ([SRE postmortem culture](https://sre.google/sre-book/postmortem-culture/)).

*see also:* [[audit ready trace digests compress incident context for reviews]] · [[incident disclosure quality is becoming a strategic asset]]

## automation pattern
Packets merge trace digests, replay evidence, and ownership metadata into a review-ready template.

## operations signal
- Postmortem turnaround time declines.
- Narrative consistency improves across teams.
- Human review remains essential for accountability judgment.

## my take
Structured packets reduce busywork and free teams to focus on root-cause quality.
