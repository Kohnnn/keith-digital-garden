# market confidence now punishes vague ai narratives

Earnings calls filled with “AI strategy” but thin operational detail now trigger immediate skepticism; valuation moves increasingly follow execution proof rather than generic narrative.

*see also:* [[ai risk stories drive market narratives]] · [[saas multiples climb as macro steadies]]

## signal vs noise
- Signal: guidance that includes deployment metrics and margin impact is rewarded.
- Signal: vague references to “future AI leverage” get discounted quickly.
- Noise: short-term pops from headline announcements without usage data.

## time horizon
Short term, narrative still moves prices intraday. Medium term, operating evidence dominates. Long term, capital flows to teams with disciplined AI unit economics.

## my take
The market matured faster than many leadership teams expected. Storytelling still matters, but in 2024 it needs receipts.
