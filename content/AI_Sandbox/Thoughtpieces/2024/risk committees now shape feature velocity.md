# risk committees now shape feature velocity

In 2024, feature timing increasingly depends on risk committee decisions, not just sprint completion. That can feel slow, but it also prevents expensive reversals.

*see also:* [[compliance teams are now product teams]] · [[ai procurement is now governance theater and reality]]

## context plus claim
When user-facing AI is probabilistic, release governance becomes part of the product system. Committees that only react late create drag; committees embedded early create predictable cadence.

## signal braid
- Teams with clear risk thresholds ship with fewer emergency rollbacks.
- Committees that publish decision criteria reduce internal friction.
- Governance ambiguity is now a bigger blocker than model quality in many orgs.

## my take
I no longer see risk review as red tape by default. Well-designed committees are throughput tools in disguise.
