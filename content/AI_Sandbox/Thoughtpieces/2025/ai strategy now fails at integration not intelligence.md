# ai strategy now fails at integration not intelligence

As model quality converges, the main differentiator in enterprise outcomes is integration quality across identity, data contracts, and workflow controls ([Thoughtworks technology radar](https://www.thoughtworks.com/radar)).

*see also:* [[ops clarity beats model novelty in sustained adoption]] · [[model choice matters less than boundary design]]

## where plans break
Roadmaps over-index on model selection and under-invest in cross-system reliability and ownership clarity.

## compounding effect
- Integration debt creates hidden cost and incident drag.
- Model upgrades fail to deliver expected value in brittle stacks.
- Teams with cleaner interfaces capture gains faster.

## my take
Intelligence is increasingly available; integration discipline is still scarce.
