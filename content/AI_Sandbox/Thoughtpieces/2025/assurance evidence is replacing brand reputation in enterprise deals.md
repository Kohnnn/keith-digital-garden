# assurance evidence is replacing brand reputation in enterprise deals

Enterprise buyers are weighting auditability, incident transparency, and control evidence more heavily than vendor brand narrative in final AI selection decisions ([Forrester trust reports](https://www.forrester.com/)).

*see also:* [[governance maturity is becoming a distribution moat]] · [[ai audit firms see demand surge after policy deadlines]]

## trust recalibration
Brand still opens doors, but assurance evidence now closes deals.

## market consequence
- Procurement committees request deeper technical attestations.
- Renewal risk rises for vendors with weak evidence practices.
- Smaller vendors can win with stronger governance transparency.

## my take
Enterprise trust is becoming evidence-native.
