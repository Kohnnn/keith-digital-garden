# data stewardship is becoming the core ai leadership skill

As model capabilities commoditize, leadership impact is increasingly tied to data stewardship: lineage clarity, rights management, and quality accountability ([World Economic Forum](https://www.weforum.org/agenda/archive/artificial-intelligence-and-robotics/)).

*see also:* [[enterprise budgets shift from model access to data contracts]] · [[dataset revocation clauses force faster index hygiene]]

## leadership shift
The hardest problems now sit in data governance and cross-team accountability, not model-access negotiations.

## strategic outcomes
- Better stewardship improves trust and adoption.
- Poor stewardship amplifies legal and operational risk.
- Data quality governance predicts reliability more than model churn.

## my take
AI leadership is becoming data leadership with production consequences.
