# enterprise ai leadership is becoming incident leadership

The strongest AI leaders are now defined by how they organize incident response, cross-team accountability, and postmortem learning loops ([DORA](https://dora.dev/)).

*see also:* [[operational humility now beats ambitious roadmaps]] · [[the biggest ai moat is now incident memory]]

## leadership pivot
Model strategy still matters, but incident leadership now determines durability of adoption and trust.

## organizational signal
- Teams with clear incident ownership sustain velocity better.
- Cross-functional drills improve recovery confidence.
- Leadership communication quality shapes internal adoption.

## my take
Incident leadership is becoming the operational core of AI leadership.
