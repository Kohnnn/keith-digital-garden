# enterprise trust is shifting from intelligence to accountability

Decision-makers now prioritize accountability evidence, auditability, and incident discipline more than incremental model-intelligence claims in enterprise buying cycles ([Forrester trust research](https://www.forrester.com/)).

*see also:* [[assurance evidence is replacing brand reputation in enterprise deals]] · [[governance maturity is becoming a distribution moat]]

## market pivot
Intelligence still opens conversation, but accountability now closes contracts and renewals.

## strategic implication
- Reliable governance narratives outperform benchmark-heavy messaging.
- Evidence quality shapes enterprise trust velocity.
- Accountability gaps create immediate commercial drag.

## my take
Enterprise AI trust is becoming an accountability market by default.
