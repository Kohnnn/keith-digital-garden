# governance maturity is becoming a distribution moat

Enterprise buyers increasingly select vendors that can prove governance readiness with repeatable controls, audit artifacts, and transparent incident handling ([ISO 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html)).

*see also:* [[safety claims without eval lineage are just marketing]] · [[managed inference startups pivot toward compliance contracts]]

## go-to-market shift
Distribution advantage is moving toward providers with credible governance evidence, not only faster feature cadence.

## market signal
- Sales cycles shorten with prebuilt assurance packs.
- Procurement risk teams influence shortlist outcomes earlier.
- Weak governance claims create renewal headwinds.

## my take
Governance maturity is now revenue infrastructure.
