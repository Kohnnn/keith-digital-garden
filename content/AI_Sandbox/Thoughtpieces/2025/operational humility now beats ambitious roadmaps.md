# operational humility now beats ambitious roadmaps

AI programs are increasingly succeeding where teams narrow scope, instrument deeply, and iterate with operational evidence rather than betting on sweeping roadmap narratives ([DORA](https://dora.dev/)).

*see also:* [[ops clarity beats model novelty in sustained adoption]] · [[ai strategy now fails at integration not intelligence]]

## the planning trap
Ambitious plans without runtime discipline create confidence early and fragility later.

## what compounds
- Clear ownership beats broad aspiration.
- Measured rollout beats symbolic launch velocity.
- Incident learning beats narrative repositioning.

## my take
Operational humility is now a stronger competitive posture than strategic grandstanding.
