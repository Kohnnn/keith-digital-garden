# policy debt is now as dangerous as technical debt

AI teams increasingly face incidents rooted in deferred policy cleanup, exception sprawl, and undocumented control assumptions, mirroring the compounding costs of technical debt ([NIST AI RMF](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)).

*see also:* [[enterprise ai policy exceptions now require expiry dates]] · [[reliability theater collapses under transparent metrics]]

## debt accumulation pattern
Temporary policy shortcuts persist under delivery pressure until they become systemic risk.

## organizational impact
- Incident complexity increases over time.
- Audit readiness declines despite feature progress.
- Recovery costs rise when policy context is unclear.

## my take
Policy debt is now a first-class engineering and leadership concern.
