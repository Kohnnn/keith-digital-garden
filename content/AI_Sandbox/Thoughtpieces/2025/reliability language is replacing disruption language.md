# reliability language is replacing disruption language

Vendor and investor messaging is moving toward reliability, controls, and measurable outcomes as AI deployments mature beyond experimental cycles ([DORA](https://dora.dev/)).

*see also:* [[market confidence now punishes vague ai narratives]] · [[trust now accrues to rollback speed not launch speed]]

## narrative transition
Disruption framing attracted early attention, but enterprise buyers now reward credible delivery and incident resilience.

## practical consequence
- Reliability metrics appear in earnings and roadmap updates.
- Buyers demand operational evidence before expansion.
- Marketing claims face faster credibility tests.

## my take
Language is following incentives: reliability now sells where disruption used to.
