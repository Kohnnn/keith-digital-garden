# resilience stories now outperform capability stories

As buyers experience real-world failures, they increasingly reward vendors that demonstrate recoverability and control maturity over headline capability growth ([SRE principles](https://sre.google/)).

*see also:* [[reliability language is replacing disruption language]] · [[trust now accrues to rollback speed not launch speed]]

## narrative economics
Capability claims generate interest, but resilience evidence sustains enterprise demand.

## practical outcomes
- Deal teams ask more about failure handling than benchmark rank.
- Recovery proofs influence renewal confidence.
- Capability-only messaging loses traction in regulated accounts.

## my take
Resilience storytelling works because it aligns with actual buyer risk.
