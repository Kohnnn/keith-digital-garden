# accountability narratives now decide enterprise adoption

Enterprise AI buyers increasingly align around vendors that can narrate accountability with concrete evidence rather than capability slogans alone ([Forrester](https://www.forrester.com/)).

*see also:* [[enterprise trust is shifting from intelligence to accountability]] · [[governance maturity is becoming a distribution moat]]

## narrative economics
Credible accountability stories reduce uncertainty in legal, security, and executive decision loops.

## market signal
- Proof-heavy narratives convert faster in regulated deals.
- Renewal risk grows for vendors with weak accountability records.
- Stakeholders now ask who owns failure before who owns features.

## my take
Accountability storytelling wins when it is backed by incident and control evidence.
