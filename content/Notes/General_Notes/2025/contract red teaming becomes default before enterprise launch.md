# contract red teaming becomes default before enterprise launch

Enterprise buyers are adding contractual red-team exercises to prelaunch checklists, requiring evidence of control performance under adversarial conditions ([NIST AI RMF](https://www.nist.gov/itl/ai-risk-management-framework)).

*see also:* [[ai procurement committees demand live control demos]] · [[agent procurement checklists now require rollback attestations]]

## what changed
Contracts now test governance behavior, not just feature claims, before deployment proceeds.

## operating signal
- Security and legal align earlier in launch planning.
- High-risk workflows receive deeper scenario testing.
- Vendors with repeatable drills close deals faster.

## my take
Contractual red teaming is becoming a practical filter for serious enterprise readiness.
