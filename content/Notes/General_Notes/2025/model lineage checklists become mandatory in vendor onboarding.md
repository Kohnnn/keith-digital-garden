# model lineage checklists become mandatory in vendor onboarding

Procurement and security teams increasingly require model lineage evidence across data sources, fine-tune steps, and release versions before onboarding vendors into critical workflows ([ISO 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html)).

*see also:* [[safety claims without eval lineage are just marketing]] · [[assurance data rooms become standard in late stage ai sales]]

## process shift
Lineage requests are moving from optional diligence to mandatory gate criteria with explicit rejection thresholds.

## operating signal
- Vendors with clear lineage close onboarding faster.
- Ambiguous provenance increases legal review time.
- Incident forensics improves when lineage is structured.

## my take
Lineage quality is becoming a practical proxy for overall platform maturity.
