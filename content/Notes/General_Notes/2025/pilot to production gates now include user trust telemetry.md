# pilot to production gates now include user trust telemetry

Teams are adding user trust indicators to promotion gates so pilot wins must translate into production confidence, not only benchmark performance ([Google UX research methods](https://research.google/)).

*see also:* [[enterprise copilots move from chat to workflow commits]] · [[latency targets are now product promises not infra metrics]]

## maturity gap
Many pilots perform well technically but fail to sustain trust under real-world variability.

## product signal
- Promotion criteria now include override frequency and complaint patterns.
- Trust telemetry influences rollout pacing.
- Teams correlate trust drops with specific policy or latency changes.

## my take
Trust telemetry is turning rollout decisions into user-centered governance.
