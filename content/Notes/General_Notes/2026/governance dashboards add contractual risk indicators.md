# governance dashboards add contractual risk indicators

Operations teams are integrating contract-linked indicators into governance dashboards so policy drift and incident patterns map directly to renewal and liability exposure ([ISO 42001 overview](https://www.iso.org/standard/81230.html)).

*see also:* [[governance scorecards now drive renewal negotiations]] · [[agent policy analytics platforms expand into board reporting workflows]]

## what changed
Dashboards are shifting from pure technical observability to joint technical-commercial decision support.

## signal braid
- Contract exposure is visible earlier in incidents.
- Renewal planning uses shared governance telemetry.
- Executive reporting becomes less narrative-heavy.

## my take
Contract-aware dashboards make governance useful beyond compliance checklists.
