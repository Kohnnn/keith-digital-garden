# vector platform pricing shifts to freshness service tiers

Vendors are beginning to price vector services by freshness guarantees and reindex SLAs, not only storage and query throughput ([Databricks research resources](https://www.databricks.com/resources/research)).

*see also:* [[vector freshness daemons improve retrieval trustworthiness]] · [[enterprise vector database contracts shift toward usage floors]]

## scene cut
As retrieval quality scrutiny rises, buyers want explicit contractual guarantees for update latency and staleness windows.

## signal braid
- Freshness SLAs become premium contract levers.
- Buyers compare vendors on reindex guarantees.
- Pricing pressure moves from raw capacity to reliability outcomes.

## my take
Freshness is moving from technical metric to commercial product tier.
