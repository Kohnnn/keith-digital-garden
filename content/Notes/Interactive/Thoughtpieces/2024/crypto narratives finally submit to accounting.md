# crypto narratives finally submit to accounting

Crypto cycles used to run on narrative velocity. In 2024, capital increasingly demanded reconciled books, clean treasury reporting, and governance clarity before extending trust.

*see also:* [[bitcoin etf approvals change treasury playbooks]] · [[otc crypto clearing nudge reduces counterparty risk]]

## context plus claim
As mainstream allocators entered through regulated products, tolerance for opaque accounting collapsed. Story-driven projects now face forensic-level diligence.

## signal braid
- Treasury disclosures became a fundraising prerequisite.
- Exchange and custody concentration risk is now discussed in ordinary investor memos.
- Projects with disciplined reporting retained credibility despite volatility.

## counter model
Some still argue crypto remains too fast for traditional reporting rigor. That view now looks costly: opacity raises funding friction more than it accelerates innovation.

## my take
Accounting discipline is not anti-crypto; it is how crypto survives contact with mainstream capital.
