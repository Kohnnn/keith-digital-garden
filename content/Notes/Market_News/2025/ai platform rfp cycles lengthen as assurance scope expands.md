# ai platform rfp cycles lengthen as assurance scope expands

Enterprise RFP cycles for AI platforms are extending as procurement teams add deeper assurance requirements covering controls, incident history, and policy lineage ([Gartner sourcing](https://www.gartner.com/en/topics/sourcing-procurement)).

## scene cut
Deal teams now spend more time validating governance depth and less time comparing raw model benchmarks.

## signal braid
- RFP questionnaires are becoming more technical.
- Vendors with mature evidence pipelines reduce cycle drag.
- Assurance scope now materially influences win rates.

## my take
Longer RFP cycles reflect rising governance maturity, not just procurement friction.
