# meta review on escalation handoff message design

Research across human-ai operations shows handoff message structure has outsized impact on recovery quality, especially under time pressure and ambiguous context ([NIST human-ai teaming](https://www.nist.gov/programs-projects/trustworthy-and-responsible-ai)).

*see also:* [[agent handoff protocols reduce enterprise escalation risk]] · [[agent override protocols become training requirement for managers]]

## evidence map
- Structured summaries improve operator decision speed.
- Confidence framing affects override outcomes.
- Missing provenance fields increase repeat failures.

## method boundary
Handoff studies require realistic stress scenarios to capture true operator behavior.

## my take
Message design is a core reliability lever in mixed-autonomy systems.
