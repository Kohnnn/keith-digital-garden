# review of policy event schema interoperability outcomes

Evidence from platform teams indicates standardized policy event schemas improve cross-tool alert fidelity, audit joins, and incident correlation quality ([CloudEvents spec](https://cloudevents.io/)).

*see also:* [[policy event streams enable realtime governance alerts]] · [[context attestation headers improve downstream audit joins]]

## evidence stack
- Shared schemas reduce integration fragility.
- Interop gaps create blind spots during incident analysis.
- Versioning discipline determines long-term schema value.

## method boundary
Interoperability benefits depend on governance of schema evolution, not only initial adoption.

## my take
Schema interoperability is becoming foundational governance infrastructure.
