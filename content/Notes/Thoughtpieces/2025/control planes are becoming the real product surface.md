# control planes are becoming the real product surface

Teams are discovering that users and buyers care as much about policy controls, audit trails, and route guarantees as they do about base model capability ([Kubernetes control plane concepts](https://kubernetes.io/docs/concepts/overview/components/)).

*see also:* [[inference routing policies become board level controls]] · [[open telemetry for llm traces matures]]

## value migration
Control planes now determine reliability, cost posture, and governance quality across model changes.

## structural impact
- Product differentiation shifts toward controls and transparency.
- Model switching becomes easier under stable control logic.
- Platform teams gain long-term leverage in roadmap decisions.

## my take
The model is increasingly a component; the control plane is increasingly the product.
