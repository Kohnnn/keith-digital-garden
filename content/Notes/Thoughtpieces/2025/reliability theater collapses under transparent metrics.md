# reliability theater collapses under transparent metrics

Teams can no longer rely on polished reliability claims when buyers now demand concrete evidence around incident frequency, rollback speed, and policy exceptions ([SRE workbook](https://sre.google/workbook/)).

*see also:* [[reliability language is replacing disruption language]] · [[governance scorecards now drive renewal negotiations]]

## why this is happening
Standardized metrics expose the gap between performative reliability and actual operational behavior.

## practical effect
- Weak operators lose trust faster during outages.
- Transparent teams recover reputation more quickly.
- Procurement decisions shift toward evidence-rich vendors.

## my take
Reliability theater works only until shared metrics become unavoidable.
