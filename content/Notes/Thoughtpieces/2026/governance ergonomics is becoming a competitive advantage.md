# governance ergonomics is becoming a competitive advantage

Control systems that are clear, lightweight, and embedded into daily workflows are driving better compliance and faster delivery than cumbersome governance stacks ([Nielsen Norman Group](https://www.nngroup.com/)).

*see also:* [[governance clarity is now a product experience]] · [[model governance now lives in release engineering]]

## ergonomic effect
When governance is easy to follow, teams comply consistently without constant escalation overhead.

## competitive signal
- Lower friction improves control adherence.
- Fewer exceptions are needed for routine delivery.
- Governance quality scales with team growth.

## my take
Ergonomic governance is one of the most underrated sources of operational leverage.
