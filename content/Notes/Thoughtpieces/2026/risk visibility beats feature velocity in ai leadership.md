# risk visibility beats feature velocity in ai leadership

Organizations are learning that sustained AI performance depends more on seeing risk early than on shipping quickly without visibility safeguards ([DORA](https://dora.dev/)).

*see also:* [[operational humility now beats ambitious roadmaps]] · [[enterprise ai leadership is becoming incident leadership]]

## leadership tradeoff
Velocity without risk visibility creates short-term wins and long-term fragility.

## strategic signal
- Early risk detection shortens incident recovery cycles.
- Visibility quality improves roadmap realism.
- Teams avoid hidden debt accumulation.

## my take
Risk visibility is becoming the operational currency of credible AI leadership.
