# Option Pricing

A compact map of the Black Scholes model and where it breaks.

## inputs
- Spot price (S)
- Strike (K)
- Time to expiry (T)
- Risk free rate (r)
- Volatility (sigma)

## greeks
- Delta, Gamma, Theta, Vega, Rho

## limits
- Constant volatility
- Log normal price assumption
- No transaction costs
- European style exercise

## extensions
- Implied volatility
- Stochastic volatility (Heston)

## use cases
- Options trading
- Risk management
- Quant strategy design
