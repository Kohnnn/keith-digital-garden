## Overall Summary
- The tape remained policy-sensitive, with inflation and liquidity setting the practical boundaries.
- Equities advanced but leadership remained concentrated
- Rates cooled from july highs yet policy language stayed firm
- Btc tracked risk-on drift while spot demand stayed selective

## Commentary
- I prioritized regime signals over narrative speed and social consensus.
- I increased exposure only when rates and breadth reinforced each other.
- I used failed follow-through as a hard warning against oversized risk.

## Rates & Liquidity
- Front-end policy pricing remained the dominant macro transmission channel.
- Liquidity quality stayed uneven, with sharp shifts around event clusters.

## Equities
- Index-level moves often masked concentration and weak internal participation.
- I favored balance-sheet quality, cash flow durability, and downside asymmetry.

## Commodities & FX
- Dollar and commodity behavior continued to set inflation and growth stress tone.
- FX dislocations remained useful early signals for broad risk transfer.

## Crypto Macro
- Crypto remained macro-coupled, especially to liquidity and real-yield dynamics.
- I demanded cleaner participation before trusting sustained upside continuation.

## Positioning & Flow
- Positioning stayed tactical, with frequent squeezes and quick mean-reversion.
- I kept position construction modular and cut quickly when signal quality degraded.

## Outlook Next Week
- Main risk: inflation or policy surprise triggers another correlation shock.
- Main opportunity: volatility compression aligns with improving breadth and funding tone.
