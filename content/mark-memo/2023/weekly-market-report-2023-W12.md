- Fed raised rates by 25 bps amid banking volatility.
- Markets priced tighter policy path but lower immediate hiking urgency.
- BTC held elevated levels as confidence rotated across asset buckets.

## Commentary
- I prioritized rates translation and breadth quality over headline velocity.
- I upgraded exposure only when follow-through survived the next macro test.
- I treated failed breakouts as risk-management signals.

## Rates & Liquidity
- Front-end rates remained the fastest policy transmission channel.

## Equities
- Index-level strength often masked concentration and fragile internals.
