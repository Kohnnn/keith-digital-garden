## Decision Boundary
- I add risk only when rates, breadth, and flow point in the same direction.
- I cut size when headline momentum outruns participation quality.
- I treat single-catalyst breakouts as tactical until cross-asset confirmation appears.

## Where I Was Wrong
- I was early on at least one move and had to wait for confirmation that came later than expected.
- I underestimated how quickly positioning can flip when rates volatility compresses.
- I can improve by writing invalidation levels before entries, not after.

## Outlook Next Week
- Main risk: If inflation momentum stalls, this relief can reprice harshly.
- Main opportunity: Further CPI/PCE cooling can extend duration-sensitive leadership.
