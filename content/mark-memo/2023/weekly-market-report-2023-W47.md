- Momentum persisted but became less forgiving on weak setups.
- Quality and profitability factors outperformed speculative tails.
- Crypto held gains but required cleaner spot sponsorship.

## Weekly Operating Map
I treated this week like a probability exercise: build a base case, define invalidation early, and update only when cross-asset evidence changed. It kept me from forcing one narrative onto every chart.

## Decision Boundary
- I add risk only when rates, breadth, and flow point in the same direction.
- I cut size when headline momentum outruns participation quality.
- I treat single-catalyst breakouts as tactical until cross-asset confirmation appears.

## Where I Was Wrong
- I was early on at least one move and had to wait for confirmation that came later than expected.
- I underestimated how quickly positioning can flip when rates volatility compresses.
- I can improve by writing invalidation levels before entries, not after.

## Outlook Next Week
- Main risk: Complacency risk rises when vol stays compressed too long.
- Main opportunity: Stable rates and broad participation can support a measured continuation.
