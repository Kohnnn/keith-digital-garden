## Overall Summary
- Risk assets ended the year on strong momentum and low headline stress.
- Macro trend looked cleaner, but seasonal liquidity limited conviction quality.
- Crypto finished firm with periodic leverage flushes.

## How I Read the Week
The key was sequencing. I mapped what moved first, what confirmed, and what failed to confirm. That process made it easier to separate conviction setups from noise-driven moves.

## Rates Before Everything
I watched rates before anything else. The tape was forgiving only when yields moved in a controlled way. Once that order disappeared, I shifted from conviction trading to strict tactical execution.

## Decision Boundary
- I add risk only when rates, breadth, and flow point in the same direction.
- I cut size when headline momentum outruns participation quality.
- I treat single-catalyst breakouts as tactical until cross-asset confirmation appears.

## Where I Was Wrong
- I was early on at least one move and had to wait for confirmation that came later than expected.
- I underestimated how quickly positioning can flip when rates volatility compresses.
- I can improve by writing invalidation levels before entries, not after.

## Outlook Next Week
- Main risk: Early-2024 macro surprises can challenge crowded year-end positioning.
- Main opportunity: If disinflation and growth balance persists, trend continuation remains plausible.
