If I had to compress the week into one line: The new year opened with softer rates but still-fragile conviction, so I leaned on confirmation over speed.

## Snapshot
- Rates cooled from late-2023 highs and supported tactical risk carry.
- Index strength outpaced internal breadth in several sessions.
- Crypto kept a constructive tone on ETF expectations but stayed event-sensitive.

## Working Thesis
I started the year with one rule: do not let last quarter's trend decide this quarter's risk budget.
My process was to confirm each move in at least two markets before adding size. Single-market strength was useful information, but not enough to upgrade conviction.

## Yield Path First
Rates still set the outer boundary for risk pricing. I only trusted upside when yields stopped forcing one-way repricing and when dollar behavior was no longer tightening conditions at the same time.
