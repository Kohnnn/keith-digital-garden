## Snapshot
- SEC spot ETF approval brought structural demand narrative into focus.
- Price response was two-way and punished late momentum entries.
- Cross-asset confirmation remained necessary despite crypto-specific catalysts.

## How I Framed the Week
I treated the ETF headline as a structural development and a tactical trap at the same time.
I treated the week as a set of conditional branches: if rates stabilized and breadth improved, I could hold; if either broke, I cut size and reset.
