If I had to compress the week into one line: Index resilience persisted, but participation quality lagged enough to keep me selective.

- Source pack: [[attachments/market/2024/W04/source-manifest|W04 source-manifest]]

## Snapshot
- Large-cap leadership kept headline indices stable.
- Broad participation remained uneven under still-restrictive policy levels.
- Crypto moved with liquidity pulses rather than independent trend sponsorship.

## Working Thesis
I avoided treating index-level calm as proof that the average setup had high odds.
My process was to confirm each move in at least two markets before adding size. Single-market strength was useful information, but not enough to upgrade conviction.
