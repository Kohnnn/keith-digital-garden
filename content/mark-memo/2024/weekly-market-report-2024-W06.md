## Snapshot
- Rates and dollar swings created uneven cross-asset follow-through.

## Regime Notebook
My edge here was doing less and being clearer about what would invalidate each trade.
I stayed focused on sequence: catalyst, rates reaction, breadth confirmation, then only tactical sizing. Whenever one link in that chain failed, I reduced gross quickly and treated follow-through as suspect.
