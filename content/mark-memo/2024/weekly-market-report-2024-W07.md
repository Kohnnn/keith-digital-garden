## Snapshot
- Inflation surprise reset timing assumptions for rate cuts.
- Rates and dollar strength pressured broad risk in the immediate reaction.
- Crypto absorbed shock better than expected but still traded as macro beta.

## Working Thesis
I cut size quickly after CPI because I trust repricing speed more than post-hoc explanations.
My process was to confirm each move in at least two markets before adding size. Single-market strength was useful information, but not enough to upgrade conviction.

## Yield Path First
Rates still set the outer boundary for risk pricing. I only trusted upside when yields stopped forcing one-way repricing and when dollar behavior was no longer tightening conditions at the same time.
