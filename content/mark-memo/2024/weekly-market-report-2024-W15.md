## Snapshot
- Inflation surprise pushed cuts expectations further out.

## Working Thesis
I prioritized capital preservation over forcing upside calls during one-way repricing.
The workflow was simple and repeatable: catalyst first, cross-asset confirmation second, risk deployment third. If confirmation disappeared, so did size.
