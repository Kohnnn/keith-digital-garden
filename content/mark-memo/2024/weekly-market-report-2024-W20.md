## Snapshot
- Inflation print came in softer and eased policy fear at the margin.
- Rates and dollar both softened enough to support broad risk appetite.

## Weekly Framework
I increased risk only where breadth and rates both confirmed, not just because the headline was good.
I treated each session as cause and effect: what moved first, what confirmed, what failed. Size followed evidence, not excitement.

## Duration and Policy
Rates remained my highest-priority filter for both equities and crypto beta. I trusted continuation only when yields stopped repricing one-way and the dollar stopped tightening conditions in parallel.
