## Regime Notebook
I moved from prediction mode to reaction mode.
I tracked sequence first: catalyst, rates translation, breadth response, then size. When one link in that chain failed, I cut gross quickly and waited for cleaner evidence.

Rates remained the main constraint variable for both equities and crypto beta. I trusted upside only when yields stopped repricing one-way and dollar behavior stopped tightening conditions at the same time.
