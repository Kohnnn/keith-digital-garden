*see also:* [[weekly-market-report-2025-W02]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W01]] · [[weekly-market-report-2024-W52]]

If I compress the week to one line: A softer inflation pulse improved sentiment, but I still needed breadth to trust continuation.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: If next inflation print reverses, relief can unwind quickly.
- Best setup: If disinflation trend persists, carry regime can broaden.

## Quick Read
- Cooling inflation eased immediate policy stress.
- Rates relief improved duration-sensitive risk tone.
- Crypto recovered with better liquidity mood.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- Direction changed less than conviction quality this week.
- I treated disagreement between assets as a reason to wait, not to guess.
- Entries improved once I stopped rewarding late-session momentum.

## Regime Notebook
I added risk where participation confirmed, not where headlines were simply positive.
I stayed with a simple chain: catalyst -> confirmation -> allocation. When one link broke, I cut exposure.
