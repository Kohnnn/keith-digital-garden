# Weekly Market Report 2025-W04

*see also:* [[weekly-market-report-2025-W03]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W02]] · [[weekly-market-report-2024-W52]]

If I compress the week to one line: Earnings setup improved index confidence, but leadership concentration still required caution.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: If leadership narrows further, index resilience can become fragile.
- Best setup: If breadth catches up, trend durability improves materially.

## Quick Read
- Earnings headlines supported benchmark-level strength.
- Participation stayed uneven beneath headline indices.
- Crypto remained constructive but event-sensitive.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- The biggest shift was how rates and risk started agreeing more often intraday.
- I moved from reactive hedging to selective adding once confirmation improved.
- I still kept invalidation tight because trend quality was not uniform.

## Weekly Thesis
I respected the trend but treated concentration as a risk, not a feature.
I mapped each session as cause and effect: trigger, rates reaction, participation response, then size. Broken confirmation meant immediate downshift.

## Yield Path
