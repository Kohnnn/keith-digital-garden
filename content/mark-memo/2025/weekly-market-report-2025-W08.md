# Weekly Market Report 2025-W08

*see also:* [[weekly-market-report-2025-W07]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W06]] · [[weekly-market-report-2024-W52]]

If I compress the week to one line: Risk recovered as rates pressure eased, but I stayed selective on late-session momentum.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: defensive tactical
- Risk trigger: If rates reverse abruptly, this recovery can lose momentum.
- Best setup: If breadth continues improving, upside can become more durable.

## Quick Read
- Rates eased enough to improve risk tone.
- Participation improved but remained uneven by sector.
- Crypto bounced with stronger liquidity impulse.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- The biggest shift was how rates and risk started agreeing more often intraday.
- I moved from reactive hedging to selective adding once confirmation improved.
- I still kept invalidation tight because trend quality was not uniform.

## Field Notes
I leaned into strength where internals confirmed, not where price alone looked clean.
I mapped each session as cause and effect: trigger, rates reaction, participation response, then size. Broken confirmation meant immediate downshift.

## Duration and Policy
Rates were still the first gate. When yields moved cleanly, I could hold; when repricing turned one-way, I switched to tactical mode.
