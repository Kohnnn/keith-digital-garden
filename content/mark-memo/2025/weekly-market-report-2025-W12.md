# Weekly Market Report 2025-W12

*see also:* [[weekly-market-report-2025-W11]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W10]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W01]]

If I compress the week to one line: A softer inflation read briefly improved risk tone, but I still required breadth confirmation.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: If inflation re-accelerates, this relief can reverse quickly.
- Best setup: If disinflation trend persists, risk carry can broaden.

## Quick Read
- Inflation relief reduced immediate policy stress.
- Rates eased enough to support selective upside continuation.
- Crypto improved with stronger liquidity appetite.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- The biggest shift was how rates and risk started agreeing more often intraday.
- I moved from reactive hedging to selective adding once confirmation improved.
- I still kept invalidation tight because trend quality was not uniform.

## Regime Notebook
I only added risk where internals confirmed the headline optimism.
I mapped each session as cause and effect: trigger, rates reaction, participation response, then size. Broken confirmation meant immediate downshift.
