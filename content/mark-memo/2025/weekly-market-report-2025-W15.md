## Decision Strip
- Bias: defensive tactical
- Risk trigger: If data stays hot, repricing pressure can deepen.
- Best setup: If subsequent data cools, this stress can mean-revert fast.

## Quick Read
- Macro surprise forced quick policy-path repricing.
- Rates and dollar pressure challenged broad risk.
- Crypto held trend support but with sharper downside tails.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- Direction changed less than conviction quality this week.
- I treated disagreement between assets as a reason to wait, not to guess.
- Entries improved once I stopped rewarding late-session momentum.

## Regime Notebook
I cut size first and explained it later.
I stayed with a simple chain: catalyst -> confirmation -> allocation. When one link broke, I cut exposure.
