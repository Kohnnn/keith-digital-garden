# Weekly Market Report 2025-W16

*see also:* [[weekly-market-report-2025-W15]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W14]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W01]]

If I compress the week to one line: Risk recovered as rates pressure eased, but I still avoided late-session chasing.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: defensive tactical
- Risk trigger: If rates reverse abruptly, recovery quality can deteriorate quickly.
- Best setup: If breadth continues improving, trend durability can increase.

## Quick Read
- Rates relief reopened tactical upside.
- Participation improved, though unevenly across sectors.
- Crypto bounced with better liquidity tone.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- The biggest shift was how rates and risk started agreeing more often intraday.
- I moved from reactive hedging to selective adding once confirmation improved.
- I still kept invalidation tight because trend quality was not uniform.

## Weekly Thesis
I bought strength only when participation confirmed it.
I mapped each session as cause and effect: trigger, rates reaction, participation response, then size. Broken confirmation meant immediate downshift.

## Yield Path
Rates were still the first gate. When yields moved cleanly, I could hold; when repricing turned one-way, I switched to tactical mode.
