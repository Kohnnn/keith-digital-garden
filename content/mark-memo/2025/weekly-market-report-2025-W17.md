## Decision Strip
- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: If rates break higher, earnings optimism can fade quickly.
- Best setup: If rates remain stable, earnings can support selective continuation.

## Quick Read
- Earnings headlines supported index-level momentum.
- Rates stability determined whether follow-through held.
- Crypto remained constructive with rotation-driven swings.

## What Changed vs Last Week

- I reduced trade count and focused on cleaner entries with clearer exits.
- Follow-through improved only where breadth confirmed headline strength.

## Field Notes
I treated earnings as an accelerator, not a replacement for macro filters.
My workflow stayed mechanical: identify the impulse, test cross-asset confirmation, then deploy risk. No confirmation, no size.
