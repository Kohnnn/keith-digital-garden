## Quick Read
- Positioning became richer in consensus themes.
- Rates stayed range-bound but unforgiving for poor entries.
- Crypto stayed bid with faster intraday mean-reversion.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- The tape looked stronger, but internals still mattered more than index optics.
- I sized up only after cross-asset confirmation appeared in rates and FX.
- I stayed tactical around data windows to avoid avoidable drawdowns.

## Weekly Thesis
I traded fewer setups and improved entry quality.
I tracked sequence first: catalyst, rates translation, breadth response, then size. When one link in that chain broke, I reduced gross quickly and waited for cleaner confirmation.
