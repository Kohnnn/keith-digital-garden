*see also:* [[weekly-market-report-2025-W23]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W22]] · [[weekly-market-report-2025-W11]]

If I compress the week to one line: A policy-heavy week rewarded translation over headline reaction.

> [!note] Moodboard
> This week rewarded patience over speed and verification over narrative certainty.

I wrote this week from the desk, not from hindsight.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: balanced / selective
- Risk trigger: If rates reject policy optimism, follow-through can fail fast.
- Best setup: Orderly rates can stabilize post-policy continuation.

## Quick Read
- Policy communication drove intraday volatility.
- Rates response separated durable moves from noise.
- Crypto remained highly sensitive to liquidity expectations.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- The biggest shift was how rates and risk started agreeing more often intraday.
- I moved from reactive hedging to selective adding once confirmation improved.
- I still kept invalidation tight because trend quality was not uniform.

## Regime Notebook
I trusted rates after the event more than comments during it.
I tracked sequence first: catalyst, rates translation, breadth response, then size. When one link in that chain broke, I reduced gross quickly and waited for cleaner confirmation.

## Rates and Liquidity
Rates remained the primary constraint variable for equities and crypto beta. I trusted upside only when yields stopped repricing one-way and dollar behavior stopped tightening conditions simultaneously.
