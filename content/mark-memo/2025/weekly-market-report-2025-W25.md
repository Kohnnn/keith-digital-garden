## Decision Strip
- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: A sudden rates reversal can weaken this rebound quickly.
- Best setup: If breadth keeps improving, continuation can become more durable.

## Quick Read
- Recovery tone improved across risk assets.
- Rates relief supported continuation.
- Crypto bounced with stronger momentum quality.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- Compared with last week, macro noise fell but positioning risk rose.
- I reduced trade count and focused on cleaner entries with clearer exits.
- Follow-through improved only where breadth confirmed headline strength.

## Weekly Thesis
I bought strength where internals confirmed, not where momentum merely looked loud.
I tracked sequence first: catalyst, rates translation, breadth response, then size. When one link in that chain broke, I reduced gross quickly and waited for cleaner confirmation.
