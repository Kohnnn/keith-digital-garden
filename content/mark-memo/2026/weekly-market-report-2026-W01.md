- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: A fast rates reversal can break early-year momentum quickly.
- Best setup: If rates remain orderly and breadth expands, continuation quality improves.

## Quick Read
- Risk tone opened constructive but selective.
- Rates behavior still decided whether upside could hold.

- The biggest shift was cleaner alignment between rates and risk in early sessions.
- I still kept size modest until participation broadened.
- Late-session momentum remained less trustworthy than morning structure.

## Field Notes
