If I compress the week to one line: A softer inflation narrative helped sentiment, but I still needed internals to confirm the move.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: If inflation expectations re-accelerate, this relief can unwind quickly.
- Best setup: If disinflation confidence persists, trend continuation can broaden.

## Quick Read
- Risk appetite improved after softer macro interpretation.
- Rates relief supported duration-sensitive setups.
- Crypto momentum improved where spot participation followed through.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- This week had better alignment between policy expectations and price action.
- I added risk more confidently where breadth improved alongside rates.
- I still treated concentration without participation as a warning sign.

## Weekly Thesis
I kept the process simple: catalyst, rates response, breadth confirmation, then sizing. If confirmation broke, so did my conviction.
