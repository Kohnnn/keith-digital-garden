# Weekly Market Report 2026-W05

*see also:* [[weekly-market-report-2026-W04]] · [[weekly-market-report-2026-W03]]

If I compress the week to one line: The month closed with decent trend structure, but I still traded as if the next macro print could reset everything.

## Decision Strip
- Bias: constructive but selective
- Risk trigger: A single hot macro surprise can punish crowded carry quickly.
- Best setup: If macro remains balanced, selective risk can continue into February.

## Quick Read
- Carry felt stable, though not immune to headline shocks.
- Rates and FX remained the fastest invalidation channels.
- Crypto strength held where liquidity stayed supportive.

## What Changed vs Last Week
- Setup quality improved when rates and breadth moved in the same direction.
- I leaned into confirmed strength and avoided over-trading noise.
- I kept a defensive fallback for event windows.

## Regime Notebook
I kept the process simple: catalyst, rates response, breadth confirmation, then sizing. If confirmation broke, so did my conviction.
